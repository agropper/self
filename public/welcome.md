**Step 1** — Collect some health records in a folder on your computer. To get them, use Apple Health PDF Export and whatever patient portals you have passwords for. You can always add more records as you get them.

**Step 2** — Click GET STARTED — MAIA will create a private AI and private knowledge base at [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com). You will be asked to edit and verify your current medications and a patient summary assembled from the records.

**Step 3** — Chat about your records with the private AI, your physicians and consultants. Nothing is shared without your express permission. You can also chat with public AIs like Claude or ChatGPT. Choose MAIA's privacy filter to use the private AI to anonymize patient and provider names before sharing the chat with public AIs.

**Step 4** — When you Sign-out, MAIA will automatically back up to the local folder and offer to delete everything in the cloud. This saves money and protects you from security issues and disruptions as we continually improve MAIA. If you keep the cloud MAIA, your deep links to physicians and consultants will keep working and you can set an optional passkey so you can access MAIA from another computer or phone.

**Note**: MAIA is free, non-commercial, open source software. I don't want to see your private records. If you need help or just a comment, please email me at [maia@trustee.ai](mailto:maia@trustee.ai).

<!-- SECTION:privacy -->

**Privacy Note:** This demo will not share your health records or chats with anyone unless you choose to share a chat with a public AI like ChatGPT or via a deep link. The demo host (agropper.xyz, me) could access your records and chats but you will be registered under a pseudonym and I have no reason to fish through anybody's records without a written request. For privacy from me as open source code and cloud hosting administrator, you should get your own copy of the open source code and a Digital Ocean account. Since I don't want to see your records, MAIA creates a maia-setup-log.pdf log file that you can email or post for help. The file has no personal information other than possibly in the file names themselves — if concerned, edit the file names of your records files.

**No Password** (Default) If you are on your own private computer, you can try MAIA without a password or passkey as long as you use the same browser each time. When you "Sign Out" of MAIA, your private records will be backed up locally and can be removed from the cloud. When you return to this MAIA welcome page, we check if your cloud records are still available. If they are not, you may restore MAIA from the local MAIA folder.

**Passkeys** (Optional) Setting a Passkey when you sign-out of a No Password account allows you to access MAIA from another computer, browser, or mobile device.

**The Wizard** Greets you when you start a new MAIA to upload and process your health records into an AI-enhanced knowledge base accessible your Private AI agent. Prepare for the wizard by collecting all the records **for one patient** in a folder on your computer. All health records can be added to start and more can be added or removed at any time. If you have an iPhone or iPad, Apple Health makes it particularly convenient to collect records from most of the patient portals that you have a password for. In the Apple Health app, use "Export PDF" to save the file to your MAIA folder. Your records will be collected in minutes instead of days and MAIA will use them to create a detailed index and will suggest a Current Medications list for you to edit and verify. The Apple Health export PDF records are convenient and well organized, but they don't have the details you get by asking for records from each patient portal and adding them to the folder.

With MAIA, public AIs like Claude or ChatGPT will not have access to your records except as part of a chat that you see and control. Doctors and other users you invite by sharing a deep link will have access to your records by asking your Private AI questions in the chat. You can disable this option in your My Agent settings.

**AI IS NOT PERFECT** MAIA gives you the opportunity to edit and verify whatever you or others might see in the chats. The Wizard requires verification for Current Medications and the Patient Summary. It is your responsibility to check the responses from Private AI as well as public AIs you choose. MAIA helps you verify by offering direct links to information in the PDF files you imported from your health care providers.

<!-- SECTION:faq -->

* **How is MAIA different from ChatGPT Health, Claude Health, Microsoft Copilot Health, Amazon Health or Perplexity Health?**
  * MAIA does not ask you to share private information with some AI you don't choose or control.
  * MAIA is open source and available for security and privacy analysis at the GitHub. You can ask any frontier AI like Claude Code to review the MAIA code or social media before you use it.
  * MAIA, and your private records are hosted separately from the MAIA code. This demo runs at Digital Ocean on my credit card. If you pay for your own hosting at Digital Ocean or an equivalent data center, I will have no access to your data.
* **Why is an open web and open source important?**
  * As AI agents become more powerful, it's important to keep them under your control.
* **What are all the files MAIA adds to my health records folder?**
  * These files back up your MAIA so you can avoid keeping private files in the cloud any longer than necessary. This also saves money.
  * The log files are useful if you need support. If you make sure your name is not in the filenames, the logs will not disclose any private information.
  * If you want more info about these files, point Claude Code to the MAIA GitHub repository (see About) and ask.
* **Is MAIA regulated by FDA or anyone else?**
  * MAIA is decision support software which is explicitly outside FDA regulation.
  * 21st Century Cures Act, Pub. L. No. 114-255, §3060(a), 130 Stat. 1033 (2016) (codified at 21 U.S.C. §360j(o)).
  * U.S. Food and Drug Administration. Clinical Decision Support Software: Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff. January 6, 2026.
* **Can my doctor use MAIA?**
  * Yes. When you share a deep link to a chat, your doctor is using MAIA under your consent and there is no FDA or other regulatory restriction.
  * Your doctor can also suggest MAIA to you or run a MAIA for you. Medical practice is not federally regulated. A doctor's employer could restrict the use of unapproved software and might require Institutional Review Board approval for MAIA similar AIs.
